Engineering 100 Learning Assistant

In Fall 2015, I was an Engineering Learning Assistant (ELA) for incoming freshmen in Computer Science. I led a class of 25 students for three days a week for the first four weeks, teaching them about things to know while in the CS program at Illinois. Their final project involved creating an online portfolio where they could upload the resumes they worked on during the course and have their contact info be available.
